Introduction

It is the aim of Everest Public School to provide the high-quality education that every student deserves. We appreciate diversity and realize that each student is unique, deserving to be treated as an individual with limitless potential. As a private English boarding school in the Madhesh Province, Everest Public School works hard to help every student find and explore new ways of learning.

We are delivering continuous quality education by bringing out the best in students so they can rise to the top at the district level. At Everest, creativity is a factor for our great learners as our team provides students the opportunity to be explorers and acquire skills.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, values, and confidence so they can succeed in life. We aim to create a space where students think freely, dream big, and develop the skills they need for the future.

Our Vision

We want our students to become responsible, compassionate, and forward-thinking citizens. Our vision is to prepare them for the real world, helping them develop not just academic excellence but also strong character and leadership skills.
